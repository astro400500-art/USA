El Salvador' Current Political State:

  • El Salvador is experiencing major political changes under President Nayib Bukele.
  • He changed rules to stay in power longer, raising concerns about democracy.
  • The country has a history of civil war and political instability.

Key Historical Event:

  • El Salvador Gained Independence From Spain On September 15, 1821.
  • El Salvador’s Civil War Ended In 1992 By The Signing Of The Chapultepec Peace Accords.
  • El Salvador Adopted The US Dollar As Its Official Currency In 2001.

Festivals of El Salvador

Festivals of El Salvador

  • Fiestas Agostinas: Celebrated in August in honor of the Divine Savior of the World, with parades, music, and traditional events.
  • Semana Santa: Holy Week celebrations with religious processions and colorful carpets made of sawdust.
  • Day of the Dead: Families honor their ancestors with altars, flowers, and traditional foods.
